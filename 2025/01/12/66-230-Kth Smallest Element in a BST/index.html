Follow up

If the BST is modified often (i.e., we can do insert and delete operations) and you need to find the kth smallest frequently, how would you optimize?

BST如果經常會需要新增或刪除node，我們可以在每個node多紀錄該node為root的subtree node count，然後在新增或刪除node時去動態修改插入或刪除路徑上node的count值。

而當我們在每個node紀錄自己在inorder traverse的位置，就可以發揮出BST的優勢：在BST搜尋target的時間為O(logn)，而這個target就是node的count。

Time complexity:

  • insert_value(value): O(H)
  • delete_value(value): O(H)
  • find_k_smallest(k): O(H)

Space complexity: O(n)

  • insert_value(value): O(H)
  • delete_value(value): O(H)
  • find_k_smallest(k): O(H)
